Jessica Livingston speaks at Y Combinator Female Founders Conference 2015.

Jessica Livingston is a founding partner at Y Combinator. She is the organizer of Startup School, the annual startup conference, and the author of Founders at Work, a collection of interviews with successful startup founders. Prior to Y Combinator, she was VP of Marketing at investment bank Adams Harkness, where she managed an award-winning rebranding of the company. Jessica has a BA in English from Bucknell.
