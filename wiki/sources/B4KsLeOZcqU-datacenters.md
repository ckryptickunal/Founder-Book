## Summary

Y Combinator is calling for startups to innovate in the data center sector, focusing on accelerating construction, reducing costs, and implementing autonomous 'lights-out' operations to support the infrastructure demands of AI.

## Key Ideas

- Current data center construction timelines are too slow for the pace of AI development.
- Innovation is needed in power infrastructure, cooling, material procurement, and project management.
- Software will eventually manage the entire lifecycle of data center planning, construction, and operation.
- Future data centers will be 'lights-out' facilities operated entirely by autonomous robots.
