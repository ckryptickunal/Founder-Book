# Want it from the bottom of your heart. Not because someone else told you to.

## Summary

Garry Tan emphasizes the necessity of intrinsic motivation and independent thinking for entrepreneurs and leaders, arguing that true innovation requires ignoring mainstream validation and external permission.

## Key Ideas

- Intrinsic motivation is the primary driver for successful leadership and innovation.
- Seeking external approval or following existing playbooks prevents the creation of groundbreaking work.
- True pioneers must champion their own ideas rather than relying on established industry standards.
- Authenticity and internal drive are more important than conforming to mainstream trends.
