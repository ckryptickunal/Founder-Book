## Summary

The video discusses the evolution of computer-aided learning, arguing that recent advancements in AI reasoning and multimodal capabilities finally make the long-standing goal of a personalized AI tutor for every student achievable.

## Key Ideas

- The historical goal of computer-aided learning dates back to the 1960s.
- Traditional online education often fails to provide personalized experiences.
- Modern AI reasoning allows for step-by-step explanations of complex topics.
- Multimodal AI models can generate animations, 3D objects, and voice explanations to enhance learning.
- High-quality, interactive AI tutoring could democratize access to personalized education.
