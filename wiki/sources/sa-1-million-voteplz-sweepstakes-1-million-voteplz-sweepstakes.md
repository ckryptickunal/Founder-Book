# $1 Million VotePlz Sweepstakes

## Summary

Sam Altman announces the launch of VotePlz, a platform designed to simplify voter registration for young people, accompanied by a $1 million sweepstakes to incentivize peer-to-peer registration efforts.

## Key Ideas

- Voter registration processes are outdated and often inaccessible to young people due to requirements like printers and stamps.
- VotePlz uses technology to modernize registration, including an iOS app that allows registration via a photo of a driver's license.
- Gamification and financial incentives are used to encourage voter turnout and registration referrals.
- The 2016 US Presidential election is framed as a critical moment requiring high civic participation.

## Entities

## Notable Claims
