## Summary

Y Combinator introduces the 'Early Decision' program, allowing graduating seniors to secure startup funding and a guaranteed spot in a future YC batch before they finish their studies.

## Key Ideas

- Students can apply to YC while still in school.
- Accepted applicants receive immediate funding and a reserved spot in a future batch.
- The program eliminates the need for students to seek traditional job backups during their final year.
- It allows founders to commit to their startup path before graduation.
