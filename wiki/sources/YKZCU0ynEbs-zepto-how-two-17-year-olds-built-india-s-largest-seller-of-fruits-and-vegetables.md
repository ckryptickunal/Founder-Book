# Zepto: How Two 17-Year-Olds Built India's Largest Seller Of Fruits and Vegetables

## Summary

A conversation between Y Combinator's Jared Friedman and Zepto co-founder Aadit Palicha, detailing the journey of building India's largest quick-commerce grocery platform. The discussion covers the company's origins as a pandemic-era WhatsApp group, the pivot from a delivery service to a dark-store model, the importance of first-principles thinking, and the role of AI in optimizing supply chain logistics.

## Key Ideas

- Start with first principles: remove constraints to define the ideal customer experience, then work backward to make it possible.
- Focus on a small group of initial users to achieve true product-market fit before scaling.
- Quick-commerce is fundamentally a logistics and supply chain business, not just a software app.
- Customer delight is the primary driver of financial viability and operational efficiency.
- Surround yourself with people smarter than you and be willing to ask 'stupid' questions to learn rapidly.
- AI can be leveraged to automate supply chain forecasting, optimize ad spend, and reduce internal software costs.

## Topics

- [[topics/quick-commerce|Quick-commerce]]: The model of delivering groceries and daily essentials in under 10-20 minutes.
- [[topics/supply-chain-logistics|Supply Chain Logistics]]: The backend infrastructure, including dark stores and automated forecasting, required to support rapid delivery.
- [[topics/product-market-fit|Product-Market Fit]]: The process of iterating based on direct customer feedback to ensure a product solves a real problem.
- [[topics/ai-implementation|AI Implementation]]: Using machine learning for demand forecasting, ad-tech optimization, and internal operational efficiency.

## Notable Claims

- Zepto is the largest seller of fruits and vegetables in India. Evidence: Stated by Jared Friedman based on platform data.
- The first 'dark store' was the co-founder's apartment. Evidence: Aadit Palicha confirms this as the origin of their inventory-led model.
- AI has allowed the company to cut software and managed service spends to near zero. Evidence: Aadit Palicha notes the internal shift to automated tools and reduced reliance on external SaaS.

## Quotes

> At the end of the day, I think it's impossible to build a financially viable, profitable business without customer delight.
> The advantage of being young and naive is that you don't know how difficult it actually is.
> Surround yourself with people that are smarter than you and just keep learning from them and keep asking.
