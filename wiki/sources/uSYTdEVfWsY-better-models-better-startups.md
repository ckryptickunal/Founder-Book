# Better models, better startups.

## Metadata

- Video ID: `uSYTdEVfWsY`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2024-06-26T17:43:22Z

## Summary

A discussion on how advancements in AI models enable B2B startups to rapidly scale revenue by integrating improved model capabilities as premium upsell features.

## Key Ideas

- Better underlying AI models allow B2B software companies to increase revenue per customer.
- End users prioritize software functionality over the specific model powering it, allowing for seamless feature upgrades.
- Startups can achieve exponential revenue growth within short timeframes by leveraging model improvements.
- AI-driven software can enable a single employee to perform the work previously requiring ten people.

## Entities

- [[entities/y-combinator|Y Combinator]] (organization): A startup accelerator that provides seed funding and mentorship.

## Topics

- [[topics/b2b-saas-monetization|B2B SaaS Monetization]]: The strategy of using model upgrades to justify premium pricing and upsell existing customers.
- [[topics/ai-driven-productivity|AI-Driven Productivity]]: The impact of AI models on workforce efficiency, enabling significant output increases per individual.

## Notable Claims

- AI models allow one person to do the work of ten. Evidence: General observation of productivity gains in AI-integrated workflows.
- YC startups saw revenue grow from $6 million to over $30 million in a single batch. Evidence: Internal Y Combinator data from the most recent batch over a 3-4 month period.

## Quotes

> Every time the model gets better you can just pass that along as like an upsell premium feature or an upgrade to the software.
> Last batch people were making $6 million a year right at the beginning of the batch and it ended up being north of 30 million by the end of the batch.
