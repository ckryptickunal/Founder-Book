# The Line Between Software and Service

## Summary

The speaker discusses the evolving nature of legal software, noting that the distinction between software products and professional services is disappearing as AI advancements necessitate a more integrated, strategic partnership model for law firms.

## Key Ideas

- The boundary between software and service is blurring in the legal tech industry.
- AI development speed requires software companies to adapt their business models rapidly.
- Category leaders in legal tech must act as strategic partners to help firms navigate industry transitions.
- Scaling headcount while maintaining organizational culture and velocity is critical for competitive advantage.
