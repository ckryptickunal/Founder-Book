## Summary

Sam Altman discusses his investment in Helion, a fusion energy company, highlighting their efficient approach, progress toward net electricity, and the potential for fusion to solve the climate crisis and improve global quality of life.

## Key Ideas

- Helion utilizes a unique, cost-efficient approach to fusion energy.
- The company has successfully built a generator that produces electricity with minimal funding compared to industry peers.
- Helion aims to achieve net electricity production by 2024.
- The long-term goal is to provide electricity at a cost of 1 cent per kilowatt-hour.
- Abundant, cheap energy could facilitate carbon capture and significantly improve global living standards.
