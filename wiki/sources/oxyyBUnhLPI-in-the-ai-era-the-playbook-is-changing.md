# In the AI era, the playbook is changing.

## Metadata

## Summary

A brief reflection on how the emergence of AI has fundamentally shifted the optimal strategy for startup ideation, moving away from traditional 'sell before you build' methodologies toward a curiosity-driven, technology-first approach.

## Key Ideas

- The pre-AI startup landscape was constrained by a saturated idea space, necessitating a focus on sales and lean methodology.
- The AI era provides a vast, unexplored frontier that rewards technical exploration and curiosity.
- Founders should prioritize living at the 'edge of the future' by experimenting with new technology to naturally discover viable startup ideas.
