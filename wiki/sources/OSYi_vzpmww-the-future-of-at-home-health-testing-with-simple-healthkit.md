# The Future of at-home Health Testing with Simple HealthKit

## Summary

Garry Tan interviews Sheena Menezes, CEO of Simple HealthKit, a startup focused on making medical diagnostics more accessible and affordable by removing middlemen and developing proprietary testing technology. The company provides rapid at-home and lab testing for COVID-19, diabetes, and STIs, emphasizing a closed-loop, patient-centered care model.

## Key Ideas

- Direct-to-consumer manufacturing of diagnostic tests significantly reduces costs by eliminating middlemen.
- Healthcare accessibility is hindered by high costs and complex supply chains.
- A 'closed-loop' patient journey is essential, ensuring that testing is followed by actionable care and guidance.
- Company culture in healthcare should be built on empathy, scientific expertise, and operational execution.
- Diverse and inclusive teams are a core competitive advantage for building products that serve broader communities.
