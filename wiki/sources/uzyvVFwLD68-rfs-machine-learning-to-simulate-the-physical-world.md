## Summary

Y Combinator is seeking founders to build AI-driven solutions that replace traditional, computationally expensive physics-based simulations with machine learning models, enabling faster predictions for complex fields like weather, fluid dynamics, and drug discovery.

## Key Ideas

- Traditional physics-based simulations are computationally expensive and time-consuming.
- AI models act as general functional approximators capable of replacing traditional mathematical models.
- Machine learning can reduce simulation time from days or weeks to seconds or minutes.
- Faster simulation speeds unlock new, previously unaddressable market opportunities.
