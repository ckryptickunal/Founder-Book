# Ignore the haters, and keep on building cool stuff.

## Metadata

- Video ID: `h3LYXvdQ3kc`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2023-07-19T21:00:11Z
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h3LYXvdQ3kc

## Summary

A brief discussion from Y Combinator emphasizing the importance of building genuine solutions for customers over worrying about critics who dismiss 'thin wrapper' startups, while cautioning against superficial 'cargo cult' AI development.

## Key Ideas

- Dismissing critics who label new products as 'thin wrappers' is essential for founders.
- Many successful companies began as simple tools or 'toys'.
- The primary focus should be on solving customer problems in an amazing way.
- Avoid 'cargo culting' or copying AI trends just because they are popular.

## Notable Claims

- Many successful businesses started as simple toys. Evidence: Historical context of companies like Dropbox being dismissed as thin wrappers on AWS/S3.
- Building a startup in a weekend is a sign of inexperience. Evidence: The speaker suggests that those who claim a complex product can be built in a weekend do not understand the depth of the work involved.
