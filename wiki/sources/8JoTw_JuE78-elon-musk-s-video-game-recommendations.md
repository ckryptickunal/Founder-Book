## Summary

Elon Musk discusses his video game preferences, highlighting his appreciation for Blizzard titles and his belief that modern game development often neglects high-quality storytelling in favor of cinematic spectacle.

## Key Ideas

- Blizzard Entertainment consistently produces high-quality gaming experiences.
- The Last of Us is cited as a standout example of exceptional storytelling in gaming.
- Modern video games often prioritize graphics and action over narrative depth, unlike older titles that relied on story due to technical limitations.
- The original Deus Ex is praised for its superior storytelling compared to its modern successors.
