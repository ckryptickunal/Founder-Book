# The Winter 2025 application deadline is in less than a week! Apply at ycombinator.com/apply.

## Summary

Y Combinator announces that the application deadline for the Winter 2025 batch is November 12, 2024, encouraging founders to apply during this period of rapid technological advancement.

## Key Ideas

- The application deadline for the Winter 2025 batch is Tuesday, November 12.
- Y Combinator has increased to four batches per year due to the current pace of technological change.
- Highly technical teams working on cutting-edge technology are specifically encouraged to apply.
- Founders only need a co-founder and an initial idea to be eligible to apply.
- Applying can significantly alter the trajectory of a startup and a founder's life.
