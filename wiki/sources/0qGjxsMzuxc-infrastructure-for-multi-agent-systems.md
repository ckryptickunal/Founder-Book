## Summary

Y Combinator outlines the growing need for robust infrastructure to support complex, multi-agent AI systems that move beyond single-threaded loops into distributed, high-throughput workflows.

## Key Ideas

- AI agents are transitioning from simple loops to complex, distributed workflows.
- Multi-agent systems enable parallel processing of large datasets using human-level judgment.
- Building reliable multi-agent systems requires solving traditional distributed systems challenges alongside new agent-specific problems.
- There is a significant market opportunity for tools that simplify the deployment, monitoring, and maintenance of agent fleets.
