## Summary

A brief advisory from Y Combinator emphasizing the importance of technical professionals recognizing their market value and urging business leaders to practice ethical, transparent compensation and management to retain top talent.

## Key Ideas

- Technical professionals often undervalue their rare skills, making them susceptible to exploitation.
- Nice individuals are at a higher risk of being underpaid or taken advantage of in professional settings.
- Business leaders must prioritize honesty and transparency to maintain long-term employee retention.
- Retaining high-quality technical talent requires creating an environment where employees feel genuinely appreciated.
