# Former OpenAI CRO Bob McGrew thinks that two jobs of the future will be genius and manager.

## Summary

Former OpenAI CRO Bob McGrew discusses the future of work, predicting that roles will bifurcate into 'lone geniuses' leveraging AI to execute complex ideas and 'managers' acting as CEOs of AI-driven firms.

## Key Ideas

- AI will act as a force multiplier for individual productivity.
- The future workforce will likely consist of 'lone geniuses' and 'managers'.
- Individuals will increasingly operate as CEOs of their own AI-augmented firms.
- AI will augment human teams rather than completely replacing all human labor.
