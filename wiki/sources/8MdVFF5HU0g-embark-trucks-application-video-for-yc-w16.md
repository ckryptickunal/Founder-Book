## Summary

The founding team of Varden Labs (later Embark Trucks) introduces themselves and their prototype, Marvin, in an application video for Y Combinator's Winter 2016 batch, highlighting their technical backgrounds in mechatronics and previous startup experience.

## Key Ideas

- The team consists of three mechatronics engineering students from the University of Waterloo.
- The founders have a history of working together on various projects and living together for two years.
- The team possesses a diverse skill set covering software, mechanical engineering, and electrical systems.
- The founders have prior experience at startups and tech companies like Khan Academy and Thalmic Labs.
