## Summary

Letter AI is an AI-native sales enablement platform that helps revenue teams accelerate deal cycles through personalized training, coaching, and content curation. Founded by Alli and Arman, the company recently raised a $40 million Series B round, having pivoted from a developer-focused tool during their Y Combinator batch to solve the inefficiency and low adoption rates of legacy sales enablement software.

## Key Ideas

- Legacy sales enablement tools suffer from low adoption and high manual maintenance costs.
- AI can bridge the gap between rapid product engineering updates and the sales team's need for accurate, non-technical product knowledge.
- Personalization is key: Letter AI uses CRM and conversational intelligence data to tailor training and content to specific deals.
- The platform is evolving into an 'essential' tool by integrating into daily workflows rather than being a static repository.
- The company is expanding into agent-to-agent protocols and MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers to allow their platform to interact with other internal enterprise AI agents.
