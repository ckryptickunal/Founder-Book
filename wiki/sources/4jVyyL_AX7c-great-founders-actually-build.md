# Great founders actually build.

## Metadata

- Video ID: `4jVyyL_AX7c`
- Channel: Y Combinator

## Summary

In this short segment, a Y Combinator representative explains that a primary red flag for startup founders is the inability or unwillingness to build their own product prototypes, emphasizing that successful founders must be technical enough to execute their own vision.

## Key Ideas

- Founders should possess the technical skills to build their own prototypes.
- Outsourcing development or relying on agencies is a significant red flag for early-stage startups.
- The ability to build is a core indicator of a founder's commitment and capability.
- No-code solutions are often insufficient for long-term scalability compared to custom-built products.

- [[entities/y-combinator|Y Combinator]] (organization): A startup accelerator that provides seed funding and mentorship to early-stage companies.

## Notable Claims
