## Summary

This video explores the convergence of intelligent AI agents, plummeting diagnostic costs, and advancements in N-of-1 genetic medicine, which together are poised to revolutionize personalized healthcare delivery.

## Key Ideas

- AI agents can synthesize diverse health data like EHRs, wearables, and genomic scans to provide personalized medical insights.
- The cost of genomic sequencing is decreasing faster than Moore's Law, increasing the availability of diagnostic data.
- Advances in mRNA and other delivery vectors are making the production of N-of-1 genetic medicines increasingly feasible.
- Regulatory bodies like the FDA are showing greater openness toward personalized medical procedures.
- A new ecosystem of startups is required to support the infrastructure of intelligent, personalized care.
