## Summary

Blake Scholl, founder of Boom Supersonic, discusses the successful supersonic flight of the XB-1 demonstrator jet and shares his philosophy on founder motivation and the importance of pursuing ambitious, personally meaningful projects.

## Key Ideas

- The XB-1 is the first independently developed supersonic jet to break the sound barrier.
- The XB-1 serves as a learning platform to develop technology for future safe, supersonic passenger airliners.
- Founder motivation is a critical, often undervalued factor in the success of a startup.
- Founders should prioritize projects that align with their personal passions to sustain them through the inevitable 'highs and lows' of entrepreneurship.
- Working on 'big' problems is as difficult as working on small ones, so founders should aim for maximum impact.
