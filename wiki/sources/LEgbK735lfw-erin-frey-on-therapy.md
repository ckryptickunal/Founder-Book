## Summary

Erin Frey, co-founder and CEO of the startup Kip, shares her personal journey with therapy, explaining how it helped her manage the intense stress and anxiety associated with running a company. She advocates for therapy as a proactive tool for mental maintenance, similar to physical checkups, and describes how it provides frameworks to navigate negative thought patterns and improve decision-making.

## Key Ideas

- Founders face predictable patterns of stress, anxiety, and uncertainty that can hinder performance.
- Therapy is a scientifically proven process that helps individuals understand their thought patterns and behaviors.
- Cognitive reframing techniques can help founders manage fear of failure and improve decision-making.
- Therapy provides a safe, confidential space to discuss problems that cannot be shared with co-founders or family.
- Mental health should be treated with the same proactive care as physical health, such as regular dental or medical checkups.
