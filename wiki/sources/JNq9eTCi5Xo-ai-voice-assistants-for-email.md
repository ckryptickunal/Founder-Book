# AI Voice Assistants for Email

## Summary

The speaker is calling for startups to develop AI-powered voice assistants capable of managing email workflows, specifically targeting productivity during commutes and accessibility for those with physical limitations.

## Key Ideas

- Leveraging commute time for email triage and drafting via voice.
- The potential for AI to achieve 'inbox zero' before arriving at work.
- The necessity of voice-first interfaces for accessibility, particularly for those with physical limitations like nerve damage.
- Email as a foundational data source for building comprehensive, personalized AI assistants.
