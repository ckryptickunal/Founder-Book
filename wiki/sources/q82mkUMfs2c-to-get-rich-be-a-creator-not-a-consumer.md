# To get RICH, be a CREATOR not a CONSUMER!

## Summary

Garry Tan argues that true fulfillment and wealth are achieved by shifting from a consumer mindset, which relies on temporary material satisfaction, to a creator mindset focused on generating value and solving problems for others.

## Key Ideas

- Consumerism is a trap that trades time for unnecessary material goods.
- True fulfillment comes from creation rather than consumption.
- Wealth is generated by creating products, finding leverage, and solving problems for others.
- Shifting focus from personal wants to serving others is the path to success.
