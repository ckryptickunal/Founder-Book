# Being accessible is one of the major advantages startups have over big companies.

## Metadata

- Video ID: `xv2XJ98MHMU`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2023-04-26T16:56:39Z
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xv2XJ98MHMU

## Summary

The video argues that startup founders should leverage their accessibility as a competitive advantage rather than attempting to mimic the impersonal, bureaucratic customer support structures of large corporations.

## Key Ideas

- Founders often mistakenly try to project the image of a large company by hiding behind layers of support staff.
- Customers value direct access to founders and the people who build the products they use.
- Accessibility and the feeling of being heard are unique advantages that startups possess over large, established organizations.
- Startups should embrace their size rather than trying to hide it.

## Notable Claims

- Startups have a competitive advantage over big companies by being accessible to their customers. Evidence: Customers prefer talking to founders and the actual creators of the software they use.

## Quotes

> People like to talk to the founder of companies who knew and they like to talk to the people that make software that they use.
> They like to feel listened to and they like to feel heard and that's unique and the big companies can't do that.
