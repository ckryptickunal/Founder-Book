# Creating the "Impossible Foods" of Dairy -- Meet Eclipse's amazing Micelle technology

## Summary

Garry Tan interviews the founder of Eclipse Foods, a company utilizing a proprietary, patent-pending micelle technology to create plant-based dairy products that replicate the taste, texture, and functionality of conventional dairy, aiming to capture the mainstream market rather than just vegans.

## Key Ideas

- Eclipse Foods uses a unique micelle formation process to create plant-based dairy that is indistinguishable from animal-based products.
- The goal is to win over mainstream consumers by matching the taste and texture of conventional dairy, similar to the strategy used by Impossible Foods and Beyond Meat.
- Plant-based dairy is a massive, untapped market opportunity compared to the current niche vegan offerings.
- The company employs an omni-channel strategy, including food service, direct-to-consumer, and retail partnerships like Whole Foods.
- Switching to a plant-based diet is cited as the single most impactful individual action for reducing carbon footprints.
- Persistence and 'cold outreach' are highlighted as essential tools for outsiders to break into new, competitive industries.
