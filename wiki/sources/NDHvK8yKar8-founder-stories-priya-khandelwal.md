## Summary

Priya Khandelwal, co-founder and CEO of Nixo, discusses her transition from a Stanford AI researcher to a startup founder, highlighting how Y Combinator accelerated her company's growth and provided a supportive community of AI founders.

## Key Ideas

- Nixo helps AI companies achieve SaaS-level margins on custom deployments and services.
- The Y Combinator community provides direct access to potential customers within the AI ecosystem.
- Founding a startup involves solving novel problems, which requires persistence and community support.
- Peer support among founders helps normalize the uncertainty of the startup journey.
