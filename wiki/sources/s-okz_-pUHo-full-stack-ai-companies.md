# Full-stack AI Companies

## Metadata

- Video ID: `s-okz_-pUHo`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2025-05-07T16:25:06Z
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s-okz_-pUHo

## Summary

Y Combinator encourages founders to build 'full-stack AI' companies, which involve using AI to perform the core work of an industry rather than just selling AI tools to existing incumbents.

## Key Ideas

- Distinction between AI-enabled software tools and full-stack AI companies.
- Full-stack AI companies compete directly with traditional industry incumbents.
- The strategy is particularly effective in industries dominated by slow-moving legacy firms.
- Founders should focus on automating entire workflows rather than just providing software to existing players.

## Entities

- [[entities/y-combinator|Y Combinator]] (organization): A startup accelerator that provides seed funding for startups.

## Topics

- [[topics/full-stack-ai|Full-stack AI]]: A business model where an AI company performs the end-to-end service of an industry instead of selling software to that industry.
- [[topics/industry-disruption|Industry Disruption]]: Using AI to bypass traditional incumbents by providing faster, more efficient services directly to the end customer.

## Notable Claims

- Founders can achieve greater impact by starting their own firms powered by AI rather than selling AI agents to existing firms. Evidence: The speaker contrasts selling AI to law firms versus starting a law firm staffed by AI agents.

## Quotes

> Instead of selling to the dinosaurs you could simply beat them.
