# This Year We Can End the Death Penalty in California

## Metadata

- Video ID: `pg-this-year-we-can-end-the-death-penalty-in-california`
- Channel: Paul Graham Essays
- Published: November 2016
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pg-this-year-we-can-end-the-death-penalty-in-california

## Summary

Paul Graham advocates for the abolition of the death penalty in California by urging voters to support Proposition 62 and reject Proposition 66, citing the high risk of executing innocent individuals due to systemic failures in the justice system.

## Key Ideas

- The death penalty debate is not about the morality of killing killers, but the risk of killing the innocent.
- Systemic issues like police error, incompetent legal defense, and prosecutor bias make the death penalty unreliable.
- Approximately 4% of death row inmates are estimated to be innocent.
- California voters face a choice between Proposition 62 (abolition) and Proposition 66 (expedited execution).
