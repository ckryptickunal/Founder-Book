# Housing in the Bay Area

## Summary

Sam Altman advocates for Governor Jerry Brown's proposed legislation to streamline housing development in the Bay Area by shifting from discretionary approval to 'by-right' permitting for projects that meet zoning and affordability requirements.

## Key Ideas

- Increasing housing supply is essential to lowering costs and reducing wealth inequality.
- Discretionary approval processes in the Bay Area allow local commissions and lawsuits to stall projects that already comply with zoning.
- The proposed legislation would mandate automatic approval for multi-family developments that include 5-20% affordable housing units.
- Public advocacy and direct contact with state legislators are critical to passing the Budget Trailer Bill.
