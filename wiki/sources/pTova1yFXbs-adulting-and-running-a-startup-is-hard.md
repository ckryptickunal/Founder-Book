## Summary

A brief reflection on the necessity for startup founders to adopt a higher level of personal responsibility and maturity compared to their peers to achieve extraordinary success.

## Key Ideas

- Founding a successful company requires a higher standard of 'adulting' than the average person.
- Comparing oneself to peers who have standard jobs is a mistake for founders.
- Extraordinary goals necessitate distancing oneself from the lifestyle and habits of the average peer group.
