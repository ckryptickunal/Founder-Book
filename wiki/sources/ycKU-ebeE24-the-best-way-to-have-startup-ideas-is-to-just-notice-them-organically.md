# The best way to have startup ideas is to just notice them organically.

## Metadata

- Video ID: `ycKU-ebeE24`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2022-12-19T17:02:22Z

## Summary

The video outlines the most effective strategy for generating startup ideas, which is to notice them organically rather than forcing the ideation process. It suggests that becoming an expert in a valuable field and building projects out of curiosity are the best ways to position oneself for future entrepreneurial success.

## Key Ideas

- 70% of YC top 100 companies originated from organic ideas rather than forced brainstorming.
- Becoming an expert in a valuable field naturally exposes individuals to problems worth solving.
- Working at an existing startup is a high-leverage way to gain domain expertise.
- Programmers should build projects based on personal interest, as these can evolve into viable businesses.
