## Summary

Encord, founded by Eric and Ulrich, is an AI-native data infrastructure platform specializing in physical AI and robotics. The company provides a universal data layer for managing, annotating, and evaluating data, helping AI teams accelerate their model development and deployment. Recently securing $60 million in Series C funding, Encord aims to become the foundational infrastructure for all physical AI data, similar to Stripe's role in financial transactions.

## Key Ideas

- Data quality is the primary bottleneck for scaling AI models, especially in physical and robotics applications.
- Physical AI requires specialized data infrastructure to handle multimodal inputs like video, sensor data, and audio at scale.
- The 'data flywheel'—integrating pre-training, curation, and post-deployment observability—is essential for bringing robots to market.
- Human-in-the-loop systems remain critical for physical AI due to lower error tolerance compared to digital AI.
- The cost of indecision is a significant, often overlooked, burden for early-stage startups.
