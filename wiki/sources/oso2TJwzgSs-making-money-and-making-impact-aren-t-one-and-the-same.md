# Making money and making impact aren't one and the same.

## Summary

A brief reflection on the distinction between financial success and societal impact, emphasizing that personal legacy and the fulfillment of daily duties are the true drivers of societal progress.

## Key Ideas

- Financial wealth does not equate to personal fulfillment or positive societal impact.
- Legacy is the primary source of long-term self-worth.
- Societal progress is driven by the collective efforts of ordinary people fulfilling their responsibilities.
- The definition of progress includes everyday actions like raising children and working hard.
