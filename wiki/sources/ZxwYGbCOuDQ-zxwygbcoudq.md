## Summary

Will Bodewes, founder and CEO of Phone AI, discusses the company's growth, their $16 million Series A funding led by Bessemer Venture Partners, and their focus on optimizing voice AI agents for businesses. The platform specializes in handling high-volume inbound calls for industries like home services and insurance, using custom LLMs and fast-inference hardware to improve performance and conversion outcomes.

## Key Ideas

- Phone AI focuses on optimizing voice AI agents for performance and conversion rather than just answering calls.
- The company differentiates itself by building custom LLMs and using specialized hardware (Groq) to minimize latency.
- Voice AI is increasingly becoming a standard, with conversational quality and accuracy now being the primary competitive bottlenecks.
- The platform uses data analytics to provide actionable insights, such as suggesting specific question changes to improve call outcomes.
- Founding a company is described as a continuous battle that requires persistence and a 'no choice' mindset.
