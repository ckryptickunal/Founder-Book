## Summary

Max, co-founder of Volley, discusses the company's position as a leader in voice-controlled gaming for platforms like Alexa and Google Home, highlighting their flagship game 'Song Quiz' and their development of a proprietary voice-gaming engine.

## Key Ideas

- Volley specializes in voice-first gaming experiences for smart speakers and voice-enabled devices.
- The company is pioneering design patterns for a new interface that lacks traditional touch or click inputs.
- Volley is developing a proprietary 'Unreal Engine for voice' to facilitate complex voice-controlled software.
- The company is expanding beyond audio-only devices to include voice-controlled screens and televisions.
- Volley is currently scaling its team and hiring across engineering, data science, and design roles.
