## Summary

Jeremy Allaire, CEO and co-founder of Circle, discusses the evolution of stablecoins from a niche crypto concept to a foundational layer for the global financial system. The conversation covers the vision behind USDC, the shift toward institutional adoption, the regulatory landscape, and the emerging role of stablecoins in powering an 'agentic' economy driven by AI.

## Key Ideas

- Stablecoins function as an internet protocol for money, enabling programmable, full-reserve digital dollars.
- The transition from consumer-focused crypto wallets to business-to-business treasury and settlement infrastructure is a major growth driver.
- Regulatory clarity in the US and globally is transforming stablecoins from a 'gray area' asset into recognized cash equivalents for financial institutions.
- AI agents will require a native economic system, with stablecoins serving as the primary medium for high-velocity, machine-to-machine economic activity.
- The future of stablecoins lies in 'invisible' UX, where the underlying blockchain technology is abstracted away from the end user.
