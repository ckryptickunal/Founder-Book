# How To Be Successful

## Summary

Sam Altman outlines 13 fundamental principles for achieving outlier success, emphasizing the power of compounding, self-belief, independent thinking, and internal motivation over traditional career paths.

## Key Ideas

- Focus on exponential growth and compounding in your career and projects.
- Cultivate extreme self-belief balanced with rigorous truth-seeking.
- Develop sales and communication skills to evangelize your vision.
- Take risks early and keep your life flexible to allow for big bets.
- Prioritize ownership (equity) over high salaries to build true wealth.
- Be internally driven rather than seeking external validation or following consensus.
- Build a network by being helpful and spotting 'forces of nature' in others.

## Notable Claims

- You get rich by owning things, not by earning a high salary. Evidence: Historical analysis of the Forbes list shows almost no one achieved extreme wealth through salary alone.
- Long-term thinking is a massive competitive advantage. Evidence: Most market participants focus on short-term results, leaving the long-term horizon under-exploited.
- Work stamina is a primary predictor of long-term success. Evidence: Observation of thousands of founders shows that extreme results require extreme effort.
