# Harj Taggar - How Startups can Compete with FAANG Companies when Hiring Employees

## Summary

Harj Taggar, a partner at Y Combinator, explains how startups can effectively compete with FAANG companies for top talent by leveraging speed, personalized attention, and the promise of meaningful, non-bureaucratic work.

## Key Ideas

- Startups can win against big tech by moving significantly faster in the hiring process.
- Personalized attention from multiple team members makes candidates feel valued and highlights a unique startup culture.
- Highlighting the risk of project cancellation in large corporations can deter candidates from choosing big tech.
- Emphasize the direct impact of work on real users as a key differentiator for startup roles.

## Notable Claims

- Big tech hiring processes can take over a month, whereas startups can move in days. Evidence: Personal experience and observation of industry standards.
- Employees at big tech companies often experience frustration due to projects being cancelled after long periods of work. Evidence: Anecdotal evidence from friends and colleagues.
