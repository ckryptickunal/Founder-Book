# Learn to truly care about your customers.

## Metadata

- Video ID: `9kEsZkkFO1s`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2023-04-21T16:41:56Z
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9kEsZkkFO1s

## Summary

A brief discussion on the importance of building genuine, empathetic relationships with customers in sales, emphasizing that authentic care is the key to long-term success and professional fulfillment.

## Key Ideas

- Effective sales requires breaking down the barrier between the salesperson and the customer.
- Genuine interest in a customer's life and business challenges leads to better outcomes.
- Sales should be viewed as a way to help customers sustain their businesses and support their employees.
- Authenticity is non-negotiable; customers can detect when care is insincere.

## Entities

- [[entities/y-combinator|Y Combinator]] (organization): A startup accelerator that provides seed funding and mentorship to early-stage companies.

## Topics

- [[topics/sales-philosophy|Sales Philosophy]]: Shifting from transactional sales to relationship-based, empathetic engagement.
- [[topics/customer-success|Customer Success]]: Focusing on how software or services can tangibly improve the longevity and stability of a client's business.

## Notable Claims

- The best salespeople are those who genuinely care about their customers as individuals. Evidence: The speaker notes that this approach allows salespeople to remember their clients fondly even after leaving their positions.
- Authentic care cannot be faked. Evidence: The speaker asserts that customers have an innate ability to distinguish between genuine interest and performative sales tactics.

## Quotes

> If you actually care about the owner and you know their life story and you can see how the software that you're selling them might help them stay in business longer... that's real.
> You can't fake that.
