# AI-Native Service Companies

## Metadata

## Summary

Y Combinator discusses the shift from AI-assisted software tools to 'AI-native' service companies that perform the work itself rather than providing software for humans to use.

## Key Ideas

- The evolution of service industries has moved from manual labor to software tools, then to AI co-pilots, and now to AI-native service providers.
- AI-native companies sell the outcome (the service) rather than the software tool.
- The total addressable market for services is significantly larger than the market for software.
- Outsourced services are the easiest targets for replacement by AI-native companies.

## Notable Claims

- The total spend on services is significantly larger than the spend on software. Evidence: General market observation regarding the scale of outsourced professional services versus SaaS spending.
- Outsourced services are easier to replace with AI-native products. Evidence: The existing infrastructure of outsourcing makes the transition to AI-based service delivery more seamless.
