## Summary

Salient is an AI-powered loan servicing platform that automates consumer lending workflows, including credit cards, mortgages, and auto loans. Founded by YC alumni, the company recently raised a $60 million Series A led by a16z. By leveraging open-source LLMs like Llama and adopting a 'forward-deployed engineer' model, Salient has scaled to process over $1 billion in loans and manages 400,000+ daily dials while maintaining strict regulatory compliance.

## Key Ideas

- AI can significantly reduce the operational costs of loan servicing, potentially lowering the cost of credit for consumers.
- Transitioning from closed-source models to fine-tuned open-source models (like Llama) was critical for scaling from hundreds to hundreds of thousands of daily calls.
- The 'forward-deployed engineer' model—where engineers work directly with customers on-site—is highly effective for B2B enterprise AI adoption.
- Success in highly regulated industries requires building deep compliance (TCPA, bankruptcy protections) directly into the software architecture.
- Relentless customer focus and direct engagement with client workflows are more important for early-stage growth than team size.
