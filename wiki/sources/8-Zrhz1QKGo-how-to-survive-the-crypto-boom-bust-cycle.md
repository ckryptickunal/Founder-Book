# How to Survive the Crypto Boom & Bust Cycle

## Summary

Chandan Lodha, co-founder of CoinTracker, discusses the challenges and strategies for building a sustainable startup within the volatile boom-and-bust cycles of the cryptocurrency industry.

## Key Ideas

- Crypto startups must survive extreme market cycles by maintaining a long-term mission-driven perspective.
- During 'crypto winters,' companies should focus on honest communication with teams and re-evaluating first principles.
- Rapid growth phases can lead to product stagnation if companies lose touch with evolving user needs.
- The 'Think Long-Term, Ship Today' philosophy balances strategic vision with the necessity of rapid execution.
- User obsession—constant engagement with customers across all company levels—is critical for maintaining product-market fit.
