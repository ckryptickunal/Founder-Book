# 6 Skills for Successful Startup Founders: Maximize your chances

## Summary

Garry Tan outlines six essential skill sets for startup founders—engineering, product, design, sales/marketing, finance, and leadership—emphasizing that while mastery of all is impossible, founders should aim for deep expertise in one or two areas and broad competence in the rest to build effective teams.

## Key Ideas

- Founders should aim to be 'T-shaped': deep in one or two areas and broadly familiar with others.
- Technical skills act as a 'moat' and are essential for early-stage startups.
- Product management is a continuous process of finding and refining product-market fit, not a one-time event.
- Design encompasses both visual aesthetics and interaction design (UX), with 'taste' being a critical founder trait.
- Financial literacy is essential for scaling and making data-driven decisions.
- Leadership is about empathy, accountability, and the ability to influence others without formal authority.
- Startups are team sports; founders should focus on building a team that collectively covers all necessary skill sets.
