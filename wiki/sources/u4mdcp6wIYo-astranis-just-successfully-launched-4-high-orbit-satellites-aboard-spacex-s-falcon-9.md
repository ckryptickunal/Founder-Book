# Astranis just successfully launched 4 high-orbit satellites aboard SpaceX's Falcon 9

- Video ID: `u4mdcp6wIYo`

## Summary

Astranis, a Y Combinator-backed startup, has successfully scaled from a small apartment-based operation to a major aerospace company, recently launching four satellites on a dedicated SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket.

## Key Ideas

- Hard tech companies can be built with limited initial infrastructure and a scrappy mindset.
- Astranis transitioned from building prototypes on Ikea desks to manufacturing satellites in a large-scale facility.
- The company achieved a milestone by securing a dedicated SpaceX Falcon 9 launch for their satellite fleet.
- Scaling operations from a single satellite to a fleet of five presents new operational challenges and learning opportunities.
- Continuous innovation is required, with the company already developing next-generation systems alongside current deployments.
