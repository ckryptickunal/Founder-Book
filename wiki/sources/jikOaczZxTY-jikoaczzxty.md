## Summary

Siti, founder of WhyHungry, details three strategic business use cases for AI agents: automating engineering bug fixes, leveraging educational content for marketing growth, and streamlining the private chef booking process.

## Key Ideas

- Autonomous AI agents can significantly reduce technical debt by fixing backlog bugs with high success rates.
- Educational content about AI, delivered in plain English, serves as a powerful marketing funnel for B2B partnerships and brand authority.
- AI can eliminate operational friction in service marketplaces by automating administrative tasks for service providers and matching processes for customers.
- Founder-led branding involves leveraging unique domain expertise to provide value, which naturally funnels attention back to the core business.
- Internal organizational shifts, including hiring and team culture, are necessary to transition to an AI-native workflow.
