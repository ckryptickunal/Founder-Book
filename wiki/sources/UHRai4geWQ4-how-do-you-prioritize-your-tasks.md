## Summary

A brief explanation of a task prioritization strategy that emphasizes completing items on a to-do list before addressing reactive communication channels like email and Slack.

## Key Ideas

- Prioritize proactive work over reactive communication
- Use a to-do list as the primary driver of productivity
- Treat meetings, email, and Slack as secondary to core tasks
