## Summary

Rishi Choudhary, founder and CEO of Castle AI, discusses his transition from a corporate 9-to-5 job to leading a startup that provides voice AI agents for major banks and mortgage lenders.

## Key Ideas

- Transitioning from corporate employment to startup entrepreneurship allows for greater autonomy and impact.
- Castle AI focuses on automating phone calls for large-scale financial institutions.
- Working directly with bank executives allows founders to influence long-term AI roadmaps.
- The startup environment offers a faster pace of product development compared to traditional corporate structures.
