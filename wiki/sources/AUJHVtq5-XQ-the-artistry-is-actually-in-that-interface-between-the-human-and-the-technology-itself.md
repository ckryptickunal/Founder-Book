# The artistry is actually in that interface between the human and the technology itself.

## Metadata

- Video ID: `AUJHVtq5-XQ`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2024-07-02T18:31:36Z

## Summary

A brief reflection on the impact of LLMs on software development, arguing that while AI may automate technical backend tasks, the core artistry of product creation remains in the human-led design of the interface and defining the product's purpose.

## Key Ideas

- AI will likely automate backend tasks like writing APIs and models.
- The camera analogy illustrates that technology changes the medium of creation rather than eliminating the need for aesthetics.
- The true value of software development lies in the human-technology interface.
- Defining what a product should do and how it should function remains a uniquely human creative endeavor.
