## Summary

Boris Cherny discusses the intense development phase of Claude Code, emphasizing the importance of building software for future LLM capabilities rather than current limitations.

## Key Ideas

- Founders should build for the LLM capabilities expected six months in the future.
- The terminal is an outdated interface that should be a starting point, not an end goal.
- Early-stage product development often requires extreme dedication and long hours to validate potential.
- Identify current frontier weaknesses in models, as these will likely be solved by future iterations.
