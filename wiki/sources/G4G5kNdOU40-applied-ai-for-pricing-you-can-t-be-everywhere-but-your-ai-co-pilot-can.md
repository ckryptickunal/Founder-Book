# Applied AI for Pricing: You can’t be everywhere but your AI co-pilot can

## Metadata

## Summary

Garry Tan interviews Pratap Bernard, CEO of Arena AI, to discuss how AI co-pilots are transforming business decision-making, specifically in pricing. By moving away from static, manual processes toward autonomous, micro-decision systems, companies can achieve significant revenue growth through hyper-personalization and human-AI collaboration.

## Key Ideas

- Business processes should move from 'set it and forget it' mechanical models to dynamic, AI-driven systems.
- AI co-pilots act as 'cyborg' enhancements for humans, allowing for millions of micro-decisions that exceed human cognitive capacity.
- The 'F-22 fighter jet' analogy illustrates how AI handles thousands of micro-corrections while the human pilot provides high-level intent.
- Trust and control are the two primary pillars required for enterprise adoption of autonomous AI systems.
- Hyper-personalization allows businesses to tailor offers to individual customers based on real-time context rather than broad, static discounts.
