## Summary

Sam Altman announces YC Continuity's investment in Quora, highlighting the platform's rapid growth, high-quality content, and the long-term vision of CEO Adam D'Angelo.

## Key Ideas

- Quora has reached over 190 million monthly unique visitors, nearly doubling in one year.
- The platform's value increases through a network effect driven by machine learning and user-generated knowledge.
- Quora served as the inspiration for the creation of YC Continuity.
- Adam D'Angelo is recognized for his rare, long-term strategic focus in the tech industry.

## Notable Claims

- Quora has the potential to contain all human knowledge. Evidence: Altman cites the platform's high-quality user-generated content and improving machine learning capabilities.
- Quora's success inspired the creation of YC Continuity. Evidence: Altman notes that funding Quora as a YC graduate led to the realization that YC needed a dedicated fund for scaling alumni companies.
