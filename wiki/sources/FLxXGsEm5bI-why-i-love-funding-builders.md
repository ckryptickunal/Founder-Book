# Why I love funding builders

## Metadata

- Video ID: `FLxXGsEm5bI`

## Summary

Garry Tan explains why he prioritizes funding 'builders'—founders with deep technical expertise—by highlighting Brian Armstrong's background at Airbnb as the ideal preparation for creating Coinbase.

## Key Ideas

- Technical founders possess a unique advantage in securing digital assets compared to traditional physical security.
- Experience in high-stakes environments like anti-fraud at a unicorn startup serves as excellent training for future founders.
- The transition from a stable, high-growth company to a startup requires the specific mindset of a builder.
- Internet money requires robust software engineering and secure systems rather than physical protection.

## Notable Claims

- Protecting internet money requires great software engineering and secure systems. Evidence: Contrast between physical security (bullets) and digital security (software).
- Working in anti-fraud at a company like Airbnb is comparable to being in the Special Forces. Evidence: The daily pressure of dealing with hackers at a billion-dollar startup scale.

## Quotes
