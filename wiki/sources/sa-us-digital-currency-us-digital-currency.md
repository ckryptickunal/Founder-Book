## Summary

Sam Altman argues that cryptocurrency is permanent and suggests that the US government should proactively create a state-backed digital currency (USDC) to maintain global financial influence and address regulatory challenges.

## Key Ideas

- Cryptocurrency is here to stay, but the dominant version may not exist yet.
- Governments can no longer stop cryptocurrency, so they should adapt by creating their own.
- A US-backed digital currency could solve issues like taxability and pseudo-anonymity while providing a competitive advantage.
- The ideal distribution model involves giving coins directly to citizens with a mix of immediate liquidity and long-term holding requirements.
- A successful state-backed currency requires a balance between government monetary policy input and decentralized network control.
