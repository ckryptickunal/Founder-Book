# Derek Thompson’s advice for founders is to follow your curiosity.

## Summary

Derek Thompson argues that because human life is fleeting, individuals should reject mass-market conformity in favor of pursuing niche, curiosity-driven work that addresses significant global challenges.

## Key Ideas

- Human life is too short to spend it conforming to mass culture.
- Producers should prioritize individuality and niche interests over mass-market appeal.
- Curiosity is a powerful compass for identifying important, long-term problems to solve.
- Founders and creators should focus on high-impact sectors like hard-tech, robotics, and climate solutions.
