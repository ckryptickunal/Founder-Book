## Summary

Garry Tan encourages content creators to consistently publish their work regardless of current engagement metrics, emphasizing that persistence, belief in one's creativity, and a strong work ethic are the primary drivers of long-term success.

## Key Ideas

- Content creators should prioritize publishing over worrying about follower counts or likes.
- The interconnected nature of social media means content can reach unexpected and influential audiences.
- A strong work ethic is a critical differentiator for long-term career growth.
- The long-term trajectory of a creator is unpredictable, making consistent output essential.
