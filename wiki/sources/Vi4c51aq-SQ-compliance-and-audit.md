## Summary

This video discusses the potential for Large Language Models (LLMs) to revolutionize the compliance and audit sectors by automating manual, time-consuming tasks like document review, policy cross-checking, and anomaly detection.

## Key Ideas

- Compliance costs are rising due to an expanding regulatory environment including GDPR, Dodd-Frank, AML, KYC, and ESG reporting.
- Traditional compliance and audit workflows are manual, repetitive, and rely on sampling rather than comprehensive review.
- LLMs can process vast amounts of unstructured data to identify issues, contradictions, and anomalies.
- AI enables continuous auditing of all company data rather than relying on limited manual sampling.
- Y Combinator is actively seeking startups building solutions in the compliance and audit automation space.
