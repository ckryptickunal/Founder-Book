# Jared predicts that in 2025, we’ll be able to have Zoom calls with AI

## Summary

Jared predicts that 2025 will see the emergence of high-quality, real-time AI video conferencing, evolving from the voice-based AI interactions of 2024 into seamless, low-latency virtual avatar experiences.

## Key Ideas

- 2024 established the viability of natural-sounding, low-latency AI voice calls.
- 2025 will focus on solving current issues with AI video avatars, specifically latency and lip-syncing.
- The goal is to pass a '3D Turing test' where AI video interactions feel indistinguishable from human ones.
- Advancements in real-time rendering will eliminate the 'uncanny valley' effect in AI avatars.
