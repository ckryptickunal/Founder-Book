## Summary

Jessica Livingston opens the 10th annual Y Combinator Startup School in 2014, reflecting on the event's decade-long history and introducing the first speaker, Ron Conway.

## Key Ideas

- Startup School has evolved significantly in production value since its inception in 2005.
- Ron Conway is recognized as a foundational figure in internet startup investing.
- Trustworthiness and hard work are highlighted as critical traits for successful investors.
