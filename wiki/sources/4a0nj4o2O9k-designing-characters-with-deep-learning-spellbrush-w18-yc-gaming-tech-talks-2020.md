# Designing Characters with Deep Learning: Spellbrush (W18) - YC Gaming Tech Talks 2020

## Summary

Corey, CEO of Spellbrush, discusses how the company uses Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) to automate and scale character illustration for games, significantly reducing production time and costs while addressing data bias in training sets.

## Key Ideas

- AI can generate high-quality character art in seconds, compared to hours of manual labor by human illustrators.
- GANs consist of a generator and a discriminator that learn through competition to produce realistic images.
- Latent space manipulation allows for fine-grained control over character expressions, colors, and styles.
- AI can be used to correct demographic biases in training data, such as increasing representation of male characters and darker skin tones.
- Building custom on-premise hardware clusters can be significantly more cost-effective than using cloud services for long-term model training.
- Spellbrush is developing the world's first AI-illustrated game.

## Topics

- [[topics/generative-adversarial-networks|Generative Adversarial Networks]]: The mechanism of using two neural networks, a generator and a discriminator, to create realistic synthetic imagery.
- [[topics/ai-ethics-and-bias|AI Ethics and Bias]]: The challenge of addressing skewed data distributions in internet-scraped datasets, specifically regarding gender and skin tone representation.
- [[topics/infrastructure-costs|Infrastructure Costs]]: The economic trade-off between using expensive cloud computing (AWS) versus building custom, on-premise GPU clusters for model training.

## Notable Claims

- AI can generate character portraits in under two seconds that are indistinguishable from human-drawn art. Evidence: Comparison of AI-generated images against professional artist work in a blind test.
- Training models on-premise is significantly cheaper than using cloud providers like AWS. Evidence: Comparison of $24/hour cloud costs versus $0.60/hour operational costs for their custom GPU cluster.
