## Summary

The 2022 Y Combinator Go-To-Market Jobs Expo features 20 high-growth startups from the YC portfolio that are actively hiring for non-technical roles such as sales, marketing, operations, and partnerships. The event provides a platform for founders to pitch their companies and attract talent, emphasizing the importance of personalized outreach and clear value propositions for job seekers.

## Key Ideas

- Startups are actively hiring for go-to-market roles despite broader economic uncertainty.
- Personalized outreach, including specific project proposals and clear calls to action, is essential for standing out to founders.
- The 'work at a startup' platform is a primary resource for finding roles in YC-backed companies.
- Many YC companies are shifting from founder-led sales to building dedicated sales and marketing teams to scale.
- High-growth startups are prioritizing candidates with experience in enterprise sales, growth marketing, and operations.
