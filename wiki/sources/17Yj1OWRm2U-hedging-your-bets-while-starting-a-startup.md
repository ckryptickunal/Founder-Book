# Hedging your bets while starting a startup?

## Metadata

## Summary

A brief advisory from Y Combinator emphasizing that startup success requires full commitment, arguing that hedging bets with multiple career options prevents the focus necessary to excel.

## Key Ideas

- Attempting to balance a startup with multiple backup career options leads to mediocrity in all areas.
- True commitment requires 'eyes wide open' risk-taking.
- Success is defined by giving your full heart to an endeavor, regardless of the outcome.
- Regret is minimized when one acts with full intention and effort.

## Notable Claims
