## Summary

Emergent, founded by twin brothers Mukund and Madav Ja, is an AI app builder that enables anyone, regardless of programming knowledge, to create production-ready mobile apps, web apps, and websites using natural language prompts. The company achieved rapid growth, accumulating 1.7 million users and over 2.5 million apps built within three months of launch, leading to a $23 million Series A funding round from Lightspeed. Emergent's core technology is a multi-agent AI architecture that handles the entire software development lifecycle, including design, testing, security, and deployment, differentiating it from platforms that only produce prototypes. The founders, with backgrounds from Google, Amazon, and successful startups like Dunzo, initially focused on enterprise AI agents before pivoting to a consumer-facing model. Their successful launch strategy involved methodical influencer marketing and a focus on retaining 'power users' – serious entrepreneurs and small business owners who find the platform a highly cost-effective alternative to traditional development. They envision AI leading to a 'billion builders' and a new explosion of ideas.
