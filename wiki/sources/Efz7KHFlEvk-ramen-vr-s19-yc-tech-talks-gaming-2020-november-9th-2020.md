## Summary

Andy, co-founder of Ramen VR, introduces Zenith, a VR-based massively multiplayer online game (MMO) designed to be a scalable metaverse, while highlighting the rapid growth of the VR gaming market.

## Key Ideas

- Zenith combines action-adventure gameplay with an open-world MMO structure in VR.
- The game features hyper-immersive combat mechanics like parrying and time-slowing.
- Ramen VR has developed an infinitely scalable backend to support a limitless virtual world.
- The VR gaming market is experiencing explosive growth, moving from $300 million to over $1 billion in one year.
- Ramen VR successfully completed the most successful VR gaming Kickstarter and secured a partnership with Oculus.
