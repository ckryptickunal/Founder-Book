# Many AI apps today are like the horseless carriages of the late 19th century.

## Summary

A discussion on the current state of AI development, comparing it to the 'horseless carriage' phase of early automotive history, where new technology is initially constrained by the design patterns of the past.

## Key Ideas

- Technological transitions often begin by mimicking old paradigms before discovering native utility.
- Early AI applications are currently 'horseless carriages'—retrofitting new capabilities into outdated software development workflows.
- True innovation in technology takes years to move beyond superficial wrappers to fully leverage unique features.
- Historical parallels exist between the early mobile app era and the current AI development landscape.
