# The 2016 Election

## Summary

Sam Altman breaks his tradition of political neutrality to endorse Hillary Clinton in the 2016 election, citing Donald Trump as an unprecedented threat to American values, the economy, and national security, while simultaneously defending Y Combinator's decision to retain Peter Thiel despite his support for Trump.

## Key Ideas

- The 2016 election is viewed as an exceptional threat to the American Republic.
- Political polarization and the lack of dialogue between opposing sides are major societal risks.
- Firing employees for their political affiliations sets a dangerous precedent for a democratic society.
- Understanding the perspectives of those with opposing political views is essential for national progress.
