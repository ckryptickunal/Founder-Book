# The AI Economy Is Stabilizing

## Summary

A reflection on the maturation of the AI industry in 2025, noting a shift from the volatile, rapid-change environment of 2024 to a more predictable, stable economic structure.

## Key Ideas

- The AI economy has transitioned from a period of extreme uncertainty to a stable, predictable market structure.
- A clear, repeatable playbook for building AI-native companies has emerged.
- The industry is now defined by three distinct layers: model, application, and infrastructure.
- AI development in 2025 was characterized by incremental improvements rather than disruptive, paradigm-shifting breakthroughs.

## Notable Claims

- The AI economy reached a state of stability in 2025. Evidence: The speaker notes the emergence of a clear playbook for building AI-native companies and a defined structure across model, application, and infrastructure layers.
- 2025 saw incremental rather than revolutionary AI model progress. Evidence: The speaker observes that while models improved, there were no major steps forward that fundamentally shook the industry.

## Quotes

> I feel like we've kind of settled into like a fairly stable AI economy where we have like the model layer companies and the application layer companies and the infrastructure layer companies.
> There's kind of like a relative playbook for how to build an AI native company on top of the models.
