# This Startup Catches Fraud at Scale

## Summary

Variant, a startup co-founded by Karim Melamed and Michael, has emerged from three years of stealth mode to announce a $21 million Series A. The company provides purpose-built AI agents that automate complex risk, compliance, and fraud detection tasks for Fortune 500 companies and marketplaces, replacing manual human review processes with self-healing, agentic systems.

## Key Ideas

- Variant builds AI agents that automate high-stakes compliance and fraud detection, such as identity verification and content review.
- The company operates in 'the shadows' because their work involves sensitive data and security, and publicizing their methods could inadvertently help bad actors.
- Variant's technology replaces a patchwork of deterministic rules and human analysts with a self-healing, agentic system that can reason over unstructured data.
- The platform integrates with internal customer systems, including scraping data from legacy UIs, to build a comprehensive graph of risk.
- The team is lean (12 people) and operates as 'AI coding maximalists,' using AI agents to multiply their engineering output.
- The founders' origin story is rooted in their experience on the fraud engineering team at Apple, providing them with a deep, mission-driven understanding of the problem space.

## Notable Claims

- Variant's AI agents can fully automate 99% of fraud and compliance triage, leaving only the most complex 1% for human review. Evidence: Karim notes that AI agents handle the bulk of triage, while the remaining complex cases require a high-quality investigative dashboard for human analysts.
- The team of 5 software engineers produces the output of a 25-person team by using AI coding agents. Evidence: Karim describes the team as 'AI coding maximalists' where every engineer manages their own team of AI agents.
- Variant's system can detect sophisticated fraud rings that traditional classifiers would miss. Evidence: During elections, the system identified state-sponsored actors by analyzing entity relationships and context across vast datasets.
