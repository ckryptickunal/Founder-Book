# We’re still in the early innings with AI.

## Metadata

## Summary

The speaker discusses the historical lag between the introduction of transformative technology, such as the smartphone, and the emergence of major companies that leverage that technology, suggesting a similar timeline for AI adoption.

## Key Ideas

- Technological shifts require a maturation period before the most valuable applications become apparent.
- Major companies like DoorDash, Instacart, and Uber emerged years after the initial release of the smartphone.
- The current AI landscape is still in its early stages of development and market integration.
