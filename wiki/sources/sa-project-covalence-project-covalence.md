## Summary

Project Covalence is an initiative launched by Sam Altman in collaboration with TrialSpark and Dr. Mark Fishman to accelerate COVID-19 research by providing infrastructure and operational support for rapid clinical trials.

## Key Ideas

- Clinical trials are a major bottleneck for COVID-19 drug, vaccine, and diagnostic development.
- Decentralizing trials to community settings or patient homes reduces hospital burden.
- Shared infrastructure and master protocols can significantly speed up trial execution.
- Project Covalence provides end-to-end support including remote data collection, telemedicine, and biostatistics.
