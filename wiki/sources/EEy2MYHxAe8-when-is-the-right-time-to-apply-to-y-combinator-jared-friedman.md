# When is the Right Time to Apply to Y Combinator? - Jared Friedman

## Summary

Jared Friedman, a Y Combinator partner, explains that the optimal time to apply to YC is as soon as you have a committed founding team and an idea you are excited to pursue, noting that traction and revenue are not prerequisites.

## Key Ideas

- The only two requirements for a YC application are a founding team and an idea.
- Traction and revenue are not required for acceptance.
- 25% to 50% of YC-funded companies are accepted at the idea stage.
- Rejection is not final; applicants are encouraged to make progress and reapply.
- There is no downside to applying as soon as the two core ingredients are present.
