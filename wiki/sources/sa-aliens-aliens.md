## Summary

Sam Altman argues that UFO sightings are likely fabrications because their designs consistently mirror the aesthetic and technological trends of the era in which they were reported, drawing a parallel to skepticism regarding startup claims.

## Key Ideas

- UFO sightings often reflect the design language of their specific decade rather than advanced alien technology.
- Extraordinary claims, whether about UFOs or startups, should be viewed with skepticism when they align too perfectly with current cultural expectations.
- The SR-71 Blackbird is cited as a rare example of design that transcends its era to remain futuristic.
