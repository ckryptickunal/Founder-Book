# Small Teams Will Build the Future

## Metadata

- Video ID: `lfrF4c_NKKs`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2025-07-16T20:10:21Z
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lfrF4c_NKKs

## Summary

The speaker argues that the next decade will be defined by the increased productivity and impact of individuals and small teams, driven by new tools and reduced coordination costs.

## Key Ideas

- Small teams and individuals will achieve significantly more in the next decade due to technological empowerment.
- Coordination costs are a major bottleneck in productivity that new tools will help overcome.
- The quality and volume of output from small entities will see a step-change improvement.
- Increased agency for individuals will lead to higher personal satisfaction and better products for society.

## Entities

- [[entities/y-combinator|Y Combinator]] (organization): A startup accelerator that provides seed funding and support for early-stage companies.

## Topics

- [[topics/productivity|Productivity]]: The shift toward individual and small-team output capacity.
- [[topics/coordination-costs|Coordination Costs]]: The friction associated with managing groups, which is expected to decrease with new technology.
- [[topics/technological-empowerment|Technological Empowerment]]: The role of new tools and resources in enabling small teams to build complex systems.

## Notable Claims

- The next decade will see a step-change in what small teams can accomplish compared to the last decade. Evidence: The reduction of coordination costs through new tools and increased individual agency.

## Quotes

> I think one of the things that will feel most different about these next 10 years versus these last 10 years is how much a single person or a small group of people with a lot of agency can get done.
