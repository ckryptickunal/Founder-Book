# How to Give AI Agents Enough Context to Be Useful

## Summary

Sachin from Skyvern explains that comprehensive context is crucial for AI agents to be effective, preventing them from producing 'slop.' He details how Skyvern, an open-source company automating tasks for healthcare, leveraged AI agents to scale to a $2M run rate with minimal human oversight. He provides specific examples of AI agents generating Product Requirements Documents (PRDs) and content marketing, highlighting that remote companies have an inherent advantage in capturing this vital context through recorded communications. Sachin concludes by outlining best practices for any company to adopt, such as recording all communications and providing agents with full access to organizational knowledge.

## Key Ideas

- AI agent effectiveness is directly proportional to the quality and breadth of context provided.
- AI agents can significantly boost productivity and enable individuals to manage multiple complex roles.
- Remote work environments naturally foster the recording of organizational knowledge, providing an advantage for AI integration.
- Companies must intentionally restructure communication and data access to maximize the utility of AI agents.
- Iterative tuning and adversarial review are crucial for improving AI agent output from 'slop' to useful.

## Notable Claims

- Skyvern scaled past 2 million run rate with one person doing all the PM, marketing, sales, and customer support. Evidence: Sachin states he personally managed these roles, attributing the success to AI agents helping him every day.
- AI agents produce 'slop' when they lack sufficient context. Evidence: Compared to new employees who try to be helpful but lack business context, agents need good instructions, context, and the ability to critique their own work to be effective.
- Remote companies have an unfair advantage in capturing organizational context. Evidence: In remote settings, context is recorded on calls or in Slack messages, whereas in-person, it's often spoken and lost.
- By giving agents access to comprehensive company data and structuring communication, AI agents can become highly effective. Evidence: Demonstrated through examples of AI agents generating PRDs by searching call recordings, Slack, Notion, and customer data, and creating content marketing posts from customer conversations, leading to tangible business results like lead generation.
