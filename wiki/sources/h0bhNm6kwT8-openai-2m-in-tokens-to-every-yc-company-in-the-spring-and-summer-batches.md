# OpenAI: $2M in tokens to every YC company in the spring and summer batches.

## Metadata

- Video ID: `h0bhNm6kwT8`

## Summary

OpenAI is partnering with Y Combinator to provide $2 million in API tokens to all companies in the current spring and summer batches, aiming to accelerate product development through high-volume token usage.

## Key Ideas

- OpenAI is providing $2 million in tokens to YC startups, not cash.
- The investment is structured as an uncapped SAFE, effectively betting on the company's future Series A valuation.
- The initiative supports 'token maxing,' where startups use high volumes of tokens to build complex products rapidly.
- This is an experimental program limited to the spring and summer batches of YC.
- Startups can use the tokens for services like Codex or the OpenAI API.
