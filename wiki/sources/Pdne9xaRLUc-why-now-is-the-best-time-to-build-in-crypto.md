# Why Now Is The Best Time To Build In Crypto

## Metadata

## Summary

Jesse Pollock (founder of Base) and Y Combinator discuss why the current environment—characterized by improved infrastructure, regulatory clarity, and the maturity of stablecoins—represents a 'golden age' for building crypto-native startups that solve real-world problems.

## Key Ideas

- Crypto is transitioning from speculative infrastructure to a practical, programmable financial layer.
- Layer 2 blockchains (like Base) provide the necessary scalability and low costs to enable consumer-grade applications.
- Stablecoins serve as the 'killer app' for global payments, remittances, and local economic empowerment.
- Regulatory clarity is lowering the barrier to entry, allowing founders to focus on product rather than legal overhead.
- The intersection of AI and crypto offers opportunities for AI agents to use crypto as a native substrate for value exchange and verification.
- Successful crypto startups should focus on solving specific, non-crypto-native problems where the technology provides a 10x improvement in speed, cost, or accessibility.

## Topics

- [[topics/layer-2-scaling|Layer 2 Scaling]]: The use of secondary protocols to compress transactions and reduce costs while maintaining the security of the base layer.
- [[topics/programmable-money|Programmable Money]]: The ability to embed financial logic directly into software, enabling automated escrow, fee splits, and global payments.
- [[topics/tokenization|Tokenization]]: Moving traditional assets like stocks, bonds, and real estate onto the blockchain to increase liquidity and accessibility.
- [[topics/creator-economy|Creator Economy]]: Using crypto to allow creators to own their content as assets and monetize them directly through open markets.

## Notable Claims

- Crypto is currently in a 'golden age' for building. Evidence: Infrastructure costs have dropped significantly, regulatory clarity is improving, and stablecoins have reached mass-market utility.
- Stablecoins are the primary driver of global crypto adoption. Evidence: They provide access to the US dollar for users in inflationary economies and enable instant, low-cost cross-border payments.
- Smart contracts can replace massive legacy financial systems. Evidence: Shopify's commerce payment protocol reduced millions of lines of legacy code into approximately 1,000 lines of smart contract code.
