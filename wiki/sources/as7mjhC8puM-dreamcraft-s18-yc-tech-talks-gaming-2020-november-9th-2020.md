## Summary

Dreamcraft, a Y Combinator S18 startup, is building a no-code platform that allows users to create, publish, and monetize games without programming or artistic expertise, aiming to democratize game development.

## Key Ideas

- Democratizing game creation by removing the need for coding skills.
- Enabling the creation of complex games like Clash of Clans or Clash Royale in weeks.
- The platform interface is designed to be as intuitive as a map editor in games like Starcraft.
- The platform has already reached over two million players since its 2018 inception.
