## Summary

Y Combinator announces Startup School 2026, an exclusive two-day event in San Francisco for elite technical students and researchers to network with industry leaders and YC partners.

## Key Ideas

- Startup School 2026 is an invite-only, in-person event for top-tier technical talent.
- The event focuses on connecting promising builders with industry icons and YC partners.
- Previous attendees have a high conversion rate into YC founders.
- The program targets students and researchers in CS, ML, applied math, and engineering.
