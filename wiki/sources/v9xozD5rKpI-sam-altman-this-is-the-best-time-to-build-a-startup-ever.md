# Sam Altman: This is the best time to build a startup ever

## Summary

Sam Altman discusses the current state of AI development, highlighting a significant 'product overhang' where model capabilities far exceed current product applications, and teases upcoming advancements in model efficiency and open-source accessibility.

## Key Ideas

- There is a massive gap between current AI model capabilities and the products built on top of them.
- The cost-to-performance ratio of AI models is dropping rapidly, with costs decreasing fivefold in a single week.
- Open-source AI models are poised to become significantly more powerful, enabling high-performance local execution.
- The current environment is uniquely favorable for building new startups due to the untapped potential of existing technology.

## Topics

- [[topics/local-ai-execution|Local AI Execution]]: The ability to run powerful AI models on local hardware, which is expected to expand significantly with upcoming open-source releases.
