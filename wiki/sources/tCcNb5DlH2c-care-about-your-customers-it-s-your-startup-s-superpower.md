# Care about your customers. It’s your startup’s superpower.

## Metadata

- Video ID: `tCcNb5DlH2c`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2023-04-13T17:50:51Z
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tCcNb5DlH2c

## Summary

A brief discussion emphasizing that deep customer care is a startup's primary competitive advantage, urging founders to prioritize solving user problems over mimicking the impersonal processes of large corporations.

## Key Ideas

- Customer care is a startup's greatest superpower.
- Startups should avoid emulating the bureaucratic or impersonal behaviors of large companies.
- It is nearly impossible to compete against a company that genuinely cares more about the customer than you do.
- Customers intuitively recognize when a company is genuinely invested in their success.

## Topics

- [[topics/customer-success|Customer Success]]: The practice of prioritizing user needs and problem-solving to build loyalty and competitive advantage.
- [[topics/startup-strategy|Startup Strategy]]: Differentiating a new business by leveraging agility and personal attention rather than imitating established corporate models.

## Notable Claims

- It is extremely difficult to beat a competitor who cares more about the customer than you do. Evidence: The speaker notes that customers can intuitively sense when a company is genuinely invested in them.
