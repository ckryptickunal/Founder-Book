## Summary

Sam Altman introduces GPT-4o, highlighting OpenAI's commitment to providing high-capability AI for free while emphasizing the transformative nature of the new voice and video interface.

## Key Ideas

- OpenAI aims to provide the world's best AI models for free to billions of users.
- The business model relies on charging for premium features to subsidize free access.
- The shift in AI development is moving from OpenAI creating end-user benefits to empowering others to build on top of their models.
- GPT-4o's voice and video mode represents a paradigm shift in human-computer interaction due to its speed and expressiveness.
