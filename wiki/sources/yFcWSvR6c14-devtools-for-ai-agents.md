## Summary

Y Combinator is actively seeking to fund startups developing developer tools for AI agents, anticipating a shift from AI assistants to autonomous agents capable of complex reasoning and decision-making.

## Key Ideas

- AI agents are evolving from simple assistants to autonomous decision-makers.
- Models like OpenAI's o1 and o3 are significantly improving agent reasoning capabilities.
- The future of software involves individuals being supported by specialized teams of AI agents.
- There is a critical need for infrastructure, building blocks, and deployment platforms to support the AI agent ecosystem.
