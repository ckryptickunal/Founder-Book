## Summary

Elon Musk discusses his daily time allocation, emphasizing that the vast majority of his work is focused on engineering and design rather than business management.

## Key Ideas

- Musk allocates approximately 80% of his time to engineering and design tasks.
- Operational business tasks at SpaceX are delegated to COO Gwynne Shotwell.
- Musk's involvement at Tesla includes both vehicle engineering and factory design.
- Musk maintains a consistent weekly presence at OpenAI.
