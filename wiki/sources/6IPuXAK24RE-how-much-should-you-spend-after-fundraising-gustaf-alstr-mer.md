# How Much Should You Spend After Fundraising? - Gustaf Alströmer

## Summary

Gustaf Alströmer from Y Combinator advises startup founders to exercise extreme financial discipline after raising a seed round, warning that most companies fail to secure subsequent funding rounds.

## Key Ideas

- Fundraising is a means to an end, not a goal in itself.
- Founders should operate under the assumption that they will never raise another round of funding.
- Set clear milestones for a 24-month period immediately after fundraising.
- Start the next fundraising process when there is at least 8 months of runway remaining.
- Only increase spending on growth (hiring/marketing) once revenue is generated, and keep it proportional to that revenue.
