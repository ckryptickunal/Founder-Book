# Make LLMs easy

## Summary

The speaker highlights the persistent technical difficulties in training large language models, noting that despite industry growth, the underlying tooling remains inefficient and prone to failure. They call for better infrastructure, such as abstracted training APIs and specialized data management tools, and invite startups building these solutions to apply to Y Combinator.

## Key Ideas

- LLM training infrastructure has not kept pace with the rapid growth of AI.
- Developers currently waste significant time on low-level infrastructure issues like broken SDKs and faulty GPU instances.
- Data management at scale remains a major bottleneck for LLM development.
- The future of software development will rely on specialized tools for post-training and model specialization.
