## Summary

David AI is an audio data research company that specializes in collecting high-quality, multi-channel conversational speech data for AI models. Founded by former Scale AI employees, the company differentiates itself from traditional data labeling services by operating as a research lab that anticipates model needs and produces proprietary datasets, rather than just fulfilling bespoke client requests.

## Key Ideas

- There is a lack of a 'common crawl' equivalent for high-quality, multi-channel audio data.
- Modern speech models require audio separated at the source to avoid channel bleed, which off-the-shelf separation tools cannot provide.
- Voice AI is expanding beyond customer support into robotics, wearables, gaming, and avatars.
- The company operates as a 'data research lab' that predicts future model requirements rather than acting as a traditional service-based labeling firm.
- Focusing on a specific vertical (audio) allows for deeper product development and repeatability compared to horizontal data integration.
