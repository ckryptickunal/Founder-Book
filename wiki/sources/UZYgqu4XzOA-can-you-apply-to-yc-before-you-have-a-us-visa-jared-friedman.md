# Can You Apply to YC Before You Have a US Visa? - Jared Friedman

## Metadata

## Summary

Jared Friedman from Y Combinator clarifies that international founders without US visas can successfully apply to and participate in the YC program.

## Key Ideas

- Non-US citizens can participate in YC without a pre-existing visa.
- The B-1 temporary business visa is the standard solution for international founders.
- YC has extensive experience assisting founders in navigating the visa process.
- Visa status should not be a deterrent for potential applicants.

## Notable Claims

- Founders do not need a US visa to apply to or be accepted into Y Combinator. Evidence: The speaker states that many founders apply and participate without having a visa at the time of application.
- Obtaining a B-1 visa for the duration of the YC program is generally straightforward. Evidence: The speaker notes that YC has significant experience helping founders secure these visas and it is rarely an issue.
