## Summary

Philip Johnston, CEO of Starcloud, discusses the growing need for specialized inference chips in space, driven by the increased launch capacity provided by reusable rocket companies.

## Key Ideas

- Reusable rockets are drastically increasing humanity's capacity to deploy hardware into space.
- There is a significant emerging market for compute capacity in orbit.
- Space-grade electronics require specific optimizations for mass, thermal management, and radiation resistance.
- Inference chips are identified as a critical component for future space-based data centers.
