# The Data Layer for the Robot Economy

## Summary

Anchord, co-founded by Eric and Ulrich, is an AI-native data infrastructure company that provides a universal data layer for physical AI and robotics. They help top AI teams create, manage, annotate, and evaluate data to ensure high-quality input for training models, accelerating deployment and improving performance. Initially focused on computer vision annotation, Anchord pivoted to multimodal and physical AI, a move validated by the market shift post-ChatGPT. The company recently secured a $60 million Series C, bringing total funding to $110 million, to expand its offerings, including an R&D facility for real-world data collection for robotics. Anchord aims to become the foundational data infrastructure for all physical AI, akin to Stripe for financial transactions.

## Key Ideas

- Anchord provides AI-native data infrastructure, offering a universal data layer for physical AI and robotics to create, manage, annotate, and evaluate data.
- The core problem Anchord solves is ensuring high-quality data for AI model training, as model performance is directly tied to data quality and even slight errors can have significant real-world impacts.
- Anchord anticipated the AI boom, starting before ChatGPT, and initially faced skepticism from investors who underestimated the AI market's potential.
- The release of ChatGPT was a pivotal moment, validating AI's generalizability and increasing trust in AI for data processing, which significantly aided Anchord's market education efforts.
- Anchord expanded its focus to multimodal AI and physical AI, recognizing the unique challenges and opportunities in collecting and curating real-world embodied data for these systems.
- Unlike digital AI (LLMs) which benefited from abundant internet data, physical AI requires specialized data collection and infrastructure, which Anchord now provides through services like its Bay Area R&D facility.
- Humans remain crucial in the loop for physical AI, particularly for handling frontier tasks and exceptions, due to the high error tolerance and potential real-world consequences of model mistakes.
- Anchord's ambition is to become the foundational data infrastructure for every physical AI company, aiming for a similar market penetration as Stripe in financial transactions.
- Key founder advice includes making decisions quickly to avoid 'indecision debt' and being adaptable to market signals while maintaining a clear long-term vision for the company's destination.
