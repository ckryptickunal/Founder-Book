# How to build your way out of a downturn.

## Summary

Garry Tan provides actionable advice for startup founders navigating economic downturns, emphasizing the 'cockroach' mindset of survival, financial discipline, and focusing on core user value rather than market speculation.

## Key Ideas

- Adopt a 'cockroach' mindset: prioritize survival and lean operations to become hard to kill.
- Focus on 'default alive' status by ensuring expenses do not exceed revenue.
- Ignore market prognosticators and media doom-mongering; focus on building products that solve real user problems.
- Use scenario planning instead of trying to predict the future with certainty.
- If runway is short, prioritize raising capital over dilution concerns to ensure survival.
- Software continues to be a transformative force regardless of temporary market valuation fluctuations.
