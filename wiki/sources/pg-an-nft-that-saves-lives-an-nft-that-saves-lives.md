# An NFT That Saves Lives

## Metadata

- Video ID: `pg-an-nft-that-saves-lives`
- Channel: Paul Graham Essays
- Published: May 2021
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pg-an-nft-that-saves-lives

## Summary

Paul Graham discusses Noora Health's innovative use of an NFT to fund their life-saving maternal healthcare programs in South Asia, highlighting the organization's proven impact and cost-effectiveness.

## Key Ideas

- Noora Health trains mothers in hospitals to provide better post-birth care, resulting in a measurable reduction in infant mortality.
- The organization uses rigorous data collection to prove they save 9 babies for every 1,000 live births.
- Noora Health is launching an NFT where proceeds directly fund life-saving interventions, with a transparent reporting mechanism.
- The NFT's value proposition is tied to real-world impact: the higher the auction price, the more lives are saved.
- The cost to save a life through Noora Health's programs is approximately $1,235.

## Notable Claims

- Noora Health saves 9 babies for every 1,000 live births. Evidence: A study of 133,733 families across 28 hospitals conducted in collaboration with Ariadne Labs.
- The cost to save a life through Noora Health is approximately $1,235. Evidence: Calculated by dividing the organization's total budget by the number of lives saved.
