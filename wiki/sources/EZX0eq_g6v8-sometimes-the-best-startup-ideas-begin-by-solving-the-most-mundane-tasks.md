# Sometimes the best startup ideas begin by solving the most mundane tasks.

## Metadata

- Video ID: `EZX0eq_g6v8`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2024-02-12T22:23:52Z
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EZX0eq_g6v8

## Summary

A Y Combinator representative explains that the most successful AI startups in their recent batches are not focused on AGI or flashy demos, but rather on mundane workflow automation using LLMs to replace repetitive human information processing tasks.

## Key Ideas

- High-value AI startup opportunities often lie in boring, repetitive back-office tasks rather than cutting-edge AGI research.
- Workflow automation involving data entry, form filling, and information synthesis is a perfect use case for LLMs.
- Many jobs involve 'hidden' information processing that is ripe for disruption by AI agents.
- Founders should look for mundane problems where humans currently act as manual bridges between different software systems.

## Entities

- [[entities/y-combinator|Y Combinator]] (organization): A startup accelerator that provides seed funding and mentorship to early-stage companies.
- [[entities/llms|LLMs]] (product): Large Language Models capable of understanding and generating human-like text.

## Topics

- [[topics/workflow-automation|Workflow Automation]]: The use of software to replace manual, repetitive tasks involving data entry and information processing.
- [[topics/startup-strategy|Startup Strategy]]: Identifying viable business opportunities by focusing on practical, unglamorous problems rather than hype-driven trends.

## Notable Claims

- The most successful AI startups in recent YC batches are focused on mundane workflow automation. Evidence: Observations from YC partners regarding the actual performance and traction of companies within their recent cohorts.
- LLMs are uniquely suited to replace human roles involving repetitive information processing. Evidence: The nature of LLMs to read, summarize, and reformat data makes them ideal for back-office tasks currently performed by humans.

## Quotes

> I know a lot of the headlines are all around like AGI and all of the fancy stuff... the stuff that I've seen in the batches that actually taking off is a little bit more mundane.
> There's so many jobs in the world that are basically very mundane information processing... it's such a perfect fit for llms.
