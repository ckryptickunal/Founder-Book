# Career trajectories are non-linear.

## Metadata

## Summary

A discussion on the non-linear nature of career paths, specifically addressing the anxiety startup founders feel when they perceive themselves as falling behind peers in traditional corporate roles.

## Key Ideas

- Startup founders often feel they are falling behind peers due to lack of traditional titles or salary progression.
- The experience gained while building a startup is highly transferable and valuable to other organizations.
- Companies often actively seek out former founders for roles like product management because of their unique skill sets.
- Career growth should be viewed as a non-linear accumulation of diverse experiences rather than a standard ladder.
