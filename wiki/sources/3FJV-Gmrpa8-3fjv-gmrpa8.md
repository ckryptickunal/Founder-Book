## Summary

Eron Wong and John Wright, founders of Alex (formerly an AI recruiting tool), discuss their $17 million Series A funding round led by Pak 15. Alex provides an autonomous AI recruiting partner that handles sourcing, scheduling, and conducting video interviews to address inefficiencies in the hiring market, such as high applicant volume and long time-to-hire metrics.

## Key Ideas

- The hiring market is currently inefficient, with time-to-hire reaching 60 days despite high applicant volumes.
- Alex acts as an autonomous recruiter that integrates with existing ATS platforms to screen candidates via voice and video.
- Staffing agencies are a primary market segment due to their high-volume, high-pain-point hiring needs.
- The AI is capable of domain-specific technical screening, such as evaluating nuclear welders or COBOL developers.
- The company emphasizes a 'human-in-the-loop' philosophy, aiming to supercharge recruiters by removing administrative burdens rather than replacing them.
- The founders prioritize a 'candidate-first' experience to eliminate ghosting and improve accessibility in the job market.
