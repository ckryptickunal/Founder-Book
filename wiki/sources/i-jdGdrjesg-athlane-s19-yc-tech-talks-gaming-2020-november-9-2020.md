## Summary

Athlane, a Y Combinator S19 startup, presents its platform designed to bridge the gap between gaming content creators and brands, helping streamers monetize their content and turn their passion into a full-time career.

## Key Ideas

- Content creators face significant challenges in professionalizing their streaming careers.
- Athlane acts as a marketplace connecting streamers with major brands for marketing campaigns.
- The platform facilitates the creator economy by streamlining brand-creator partnerships.
- Athlane is actively recruiting for engineering, operations, and customer-facing roles.
