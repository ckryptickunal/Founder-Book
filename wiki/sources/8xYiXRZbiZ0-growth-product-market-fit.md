# Growth = Product Market Fit

## Metadata

- Video ID: `8xYiXRZbiZ0`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2022-12-30T16:52:46Z

## Summary

Divya, a Y Combinator visiting partner and two-time founder, explains that growth is the primary indicator of product-market fit, arguing that it serves as the only reliable signal that a product is truly desired and worth paying for.

## Key Ideas

- Growth is the definitive signal of product-market fit.
- Product-market fit is validated when people want a product and are willing to pay for it.
- Growth is a natural outcome of creating something that provides genuine value to a large audience.

## Topics

- [[topics/product-market-fit|Product-Market Fit]]: The state where a product satisfies a strong market demand and is validated by consistent growth.

## Notable Claims

- Growth is the only true signal of product-market fit. Evidence: The speaker asserts that without growth, other indicators are insufficient to prove that a product is desired by the market.

## Quotes

> Growth is the only true signal that you have product Market fit.
