## Summary

Sam Altman outlines upcoming updates for the Sora video generation model, focusing on providing rightsholders with granular control over character usage and introducing a revenue-sharing model to address high generation costs.

## Key Ideas

- Implementation of granular controls for rightsholders regarding character generation.
- Introduction of a revenue-sharing model for rightsholders whose characters are used in user-generated content.
- Acknowledgment of high user demand and generation volume exceeding initial expectations.
- Commitment to rapid iteration and consistent policy application across all products.
