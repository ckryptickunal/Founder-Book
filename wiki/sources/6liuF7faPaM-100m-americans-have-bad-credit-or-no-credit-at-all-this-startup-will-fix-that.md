# 100M Americans have bad credit or no credit at all. This startup will fix that.

## Summary

Garry Tan discusses Empower, a fintech startup aiming to democratize credit access for the 100 million Americans who are credit invisible or unscorable. By leveraging alternative data like banking and payroll information rather than traditional FICO scores, Empower provides instant credit access to help individuals achieve financial stability and social mobility.

## Key Ideas

- Traditional credit scoring (FICO) excludes millions of people and is prone to errors.
- Credit scores impact more than just loans; they affect housing, insurance, and employment opportunities.
- Empower uses alternative data (banking, payroll, digital wallets) to underwrite individuals who lack traditional credit history.
- Access to credit is a fundamental driver of social mobility and economic prosperity.
- Software and machine learning can replace outdated, exclusionary financial systems with real-time, fair underwriting.
