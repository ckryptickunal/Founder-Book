## Summary

The video argues that the primary obstacle to AI automation in business is not model capability, but the fragmentation of domain knowledge. It proposes a 'company brain'—a system that aggregates, structures, and maintains internal knowledge into executable skill files for AI agents.

## Key Ideas

- AI models are sufficiently advanced; the bottleneck is now internal domain knowledge.
- Company knowledge is currently scattered across emails, Slack, tickets, and human memory.
- A 'company brain' is a new primitive that transforms raw data into executable skills for AI.
- This system acts as a living map of business processes rather than a simple search or chatbot interface.
- The company brain serves as the essential layer between raw data and reliable AI automation.
