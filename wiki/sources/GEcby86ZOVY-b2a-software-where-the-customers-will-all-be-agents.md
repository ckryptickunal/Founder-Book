# B2A: Software Where the Customers Will All Be Agents

## Metadata

- Video ID: `GEcby86ZOVY`
- Channel: Y Combinator
- Published: 2025-02-04T17:28:42Z
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GEcby86ZOVY

## Summary

Y Combinator announces a new investment focus on 'B2A' (Business-to-Agent) software, where companies build products specifically designed for AI agents as primary customers rather than humans.

## Key Ideas

- The shift from human-centric software to agent-centric software.
- Current internet traffic is already heavily composed of machines impersonating humans to navigate interfaces.
- B2A software treats AI agents as first-class citizens with dedicated documentation and support.
- Future internet infrastructure will mirror the stock market, where humans and AI agents operate side-by-side.

## Entities

- [[entities/y-combinator|Y Combinator]] (organization): A startup accelerator that provides seed funding for startups.

## Topics

- [[topics/b2a-business-to-agent|B2A (Business-to-Agent)]]: A business model where software services are built specifically for AI agents to consume, rather than human users.
- [[topics/ai-agents|AI Agents]]: Autonomous programs capable of performing tasks, scraping data, and executing transactions on behalf of users.

## Notable Claims

- A significant percentage of current internet traffic is already machines impersonating humans. Evidence: Observation of bots scraping data and filling out forms to navigate human-centric web interfaces.
- The future of the internet will resemble the stock market, with humans and AI agents trading and interacting in the same environment. Evidence: The existing precedent of algorithmic trading coexisting with human traders in financial markets.

## Quotes

> We'd like to fund companies building software that assumes their customers are going to be AI agents rather than humans.
> Agents are actively supported and documented rather than an edge case impersonating humans.
