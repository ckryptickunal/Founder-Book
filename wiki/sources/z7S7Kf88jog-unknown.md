## Summary

The founder of ShopTiques shares her journey of building a two-sided marketplace for unique boutiques, emphasizing the importance of user research, persistence, and making unconventional leadership choices as a non-technical founder.

## Key Ideas

- Talk to users extensively before building a solution to ensure product-market fit.
- Always ask for opportunities; if you don't ask, the answer is always no.
- Be honest about the scale of the business you want to build and choose investors accordingly.
- It is acceptable for non-technical founders to hire a CTO rather than forcing a technical co-founder relationship.
- Marketplaces often face a two-year period of slow growth before hitting an inflection point.
- Passion and belief in the mission are essential for surviving the difficult moments of startup life.
