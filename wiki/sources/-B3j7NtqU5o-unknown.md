## Summary

Y Combinator expresses interest in funding startups developing AI-powered personal assistants that move beyond simple task tracking to autonomous execution of professional workflows.

## Key Ideas

- Transitioning from passive to-do lists to active done lists using LLMs.
- AI assistants should possess deep context of user communication, routines, and project history.
- The goal is to automate complex administrative tasks like email drafting, scheduling, and recurring process management.
- AI agents should function similarly to a human Chief of Staff to maximize user productivity.

## Notable Claims

- LLMs have the potential to act as a Chief of Staff for individuals. Evidence: The speaker describes an AI that understands communication history and scheduling preferences to take action on behalf of the user.
