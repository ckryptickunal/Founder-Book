## Summary

Cathy Di, a former Princeton CS student, shares her journey of dropping out to co-found Dedalus Labs, an agent deployment platform, after participating in the Y Combinator Summer batch.

## Key Ideas

- The decision to prioritize personal fulfillment over a traditional career path in big tech.
- The importance of having a clear vision even when lacking initial revenue or customers.
- The value of the Y Combinator community and alumni network for early-stage founders.
- The urgency of starting a startup rather than waiting for the 'perfect' time.
