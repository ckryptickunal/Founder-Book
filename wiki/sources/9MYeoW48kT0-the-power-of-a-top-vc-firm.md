# The power of a top VC firm

## Summary

Garry Tan explains the 'winner-take-all' nature of the venture capital industry, highlighting how top-tier VC firms provide startups with significant advantages in hiring, fundraising, and market success.

## Key Ideas

- Venture capital functions similarly to elite university prestige, where the brand of the investor significantly impacts a startup's trajectory.
- Top VC firms act as a signal that attracts high-quality talent and follow-on capital.
- While brand prestige is important, the ultimate measure of a VC firm's value is its financial returns.

## Notable Claims

- Startups funded by top VC firms perform better than those that are not. Evidence: Top firms enable startups to hire better talent and raise more capital from subsequent investors.
- The venture capital industry is a winner-take-all market. Evidence: The compounding advantages of brand prestige and network effects create a disparity between top firms and others.
