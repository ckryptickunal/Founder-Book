## Summary

Fei-Fei Li discusses the importance of 'intellectual fearlessness' as a primary hiring criterion for success, emphasizing that a willingness to tackle difficult problems is more important than a candidate's background.

## Key Ideas

- Intellectual fearlessness is a core trait of successful individuals.
- Background and origin are secondary to the courage to solve hard problems.
- Hiring practices at World Labs prioritize this specific mindset.
