# Learning vs Earning: How to Earn

## Summary

Garry Tan discusses the distinction between learning and earning, arguing that equity ownership in a company you build is the most effective path to true wealth, while acknowledging the high compensation potential of working for major tech firms.

## Key Ideas

- Equity ownership is the highest form of earning because it involves creating value from nothing.
- High-paying tech jobs at companies like Google and Facebook offer significant cash and stock compensation.
- Revenue per employee is a key metric for understanding why top-tier tech companies can afford massive salaries.
- The ultimate risk in a career is earning money without simultaneously learning new skills.
