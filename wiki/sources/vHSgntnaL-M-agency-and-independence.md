## Summary

A brief reflection on the shifting value of human education in the age of AI, emphasizing that as AI becomes superior at following instructions, humans must pivot toward developing agency and independence to remain relevant.

## Key Ideas

- AI is becoming highly proficient at following instructions reliably.
- Human competitiveness based solely on instruction-following is diminishing.
- The value of a college education must shift from rote test-taking to cultivating personal agency.
- Agency and independence are the critical skills for success in a post-AI world.
