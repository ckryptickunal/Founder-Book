# Jeremy Allaire

## Overview

Chairman, CEO, and co-founder of Circle. A serial entrepreneur with a background in early internet infrastructure and previous public companies, including being head of product at Macromedia. He became enamored with crypto in 2012 and founded Circle with the vision of 'HTTP for money'.
