# The Pie Fallacy

## Overview

The incorrect assumption that the economy is a zero-sum game where the rich get richer only by taking from the poor.

- [[sources/pg-economic-inequality-economic-inequality|Economic Inequality]]: The incorrect assumption that the economy is a zero-sum game where the rich get richer only by taking from the poor.
