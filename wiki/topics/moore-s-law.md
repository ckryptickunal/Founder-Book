# Moore's Law

## Overview

The observation that computing power increases exponentially, which Graham argues will make machine efficiency less critical over time.

## Source Mentions

- [[sources/pg-the-hundred-year-language-the-hundred-year-language|The Hundred-Year Language]]: The observation that computing power increases exponentially, which Graham argues will make machine efficiency less critical over time.
